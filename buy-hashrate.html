Buy Hashrate Guide

Welcome to TrustDollar Cloud Mining — here’s everything you need to know before purchasing a Cloud Mining Advance Plan through the Buy Hashrate system.

✅ How to Buy a Plan

  • Make sure you have enough balance in your wallet.
  • You can only buy up to your lifetime deposit limit.
  • Select your plan and click Buy to confirm.
  • Your plan becomes Active instantly and starts generating daily profit.

💹 How Daily Profit Works

  • You’ll continue receiving daily profit and commission until your plan duration ends.
  • Every 24 hours, click the “Collect Profit” button to transfer profit to your wallet.
  • After completion, only the earned profit will be credited to your wallet. The initial investment amount is non-refundable. If you want your investment returned, it is available only up to Plan 3. Plans above this level do not include investment return.

✅ Profit Calculation (Plan 4 – Plan 13)

  • Plans from Plan 4 to Plan 13 provide daily profit only.
  • The initial investment amount is not returned for these plans.
  • Your daily profit percentage depends on the selected plan (2.0% – 2.9%).
  • Profit is generated every 24 hours and can be collected by clicking the “Collect Profit” button.
  • You will continue earning daily profit until the plan duration ends.
  • Example: If you invest $1100 in Plan 4 (2.0% daily), you will receive about $22 profit per day during the plan duration.

✅ Referral Income System (3 Levels)

  • Level 1: 17% of your direct partner’s profit.
  • Level 2: 8% of your second-level partner’s profit.
  • Level 3: 5% of your third-level partner’s profit.
  • Referral income continues daily while your plan is active.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • You cannot buy a new plan until your current one completes.
  • Profit percentage is based on your selected plan (2.1% – 5%).
  • Even if you close the app, your plan continues automatically.
