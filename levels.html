Level Breakdown

Levels are designed to reflect your network growth. As your team expands, additional benefits and rewards become available automatically.
Level 1
Entry Level
This level includes users who join directly using your referral. Rewards are calculated based on verified participation.
Typical range: 1 – 10 users
Level 2
Growth Level
Level 2 includes users invited by your Level 1 members. Reward potential increases as network activity grows.
Typical range: 11 – 50 users
Level 3
Expansion Level
This level represents deeper network expansion. System rewards are applied automatically based on rules.
Typical range: 50+ users
Level benefits are managed by the system and update automatically. No manual action is required to progress between levels.
