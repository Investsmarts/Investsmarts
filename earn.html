Daily Earnings from Staking

TrustDollar V2 offers a transparent earning model through crypto staking. Every user earns 1% daily return on their staked amount.

✅ Example: If you stake 100 USDT, you will earn 1 USDT every day.

How Earnings Work

Once you stake funds, our system locks them and starts generating fixed daily returns. Rewards are automatically updated in your wallet balance.

  • Daily Return: 1% per day
  • Payout: Credited directly to your wallet
  • Withdrawal: Available anytime as per platform rules

Referral Income

In addition to daily staking income, you can earn through referrals:

  • Level 1: 17% of your direct referral’s staking
  • Level 2: 8% bonus
  • Level 3: 5% bonus

Transparency

All transactions are recorded securely on blockchain. You can track your staking, earnings, and referrals inside your dashboard.

Disclaimer: Earnings are fixed at 1% daily but crypto investments always involve risk. Do not invest more than you can afford to lose.
