Mining System Overview
How Mining Works

Mining starts when the user activates the mining process. Earnings are generated automatically based on system-defined activity cycles.

Mining Status

Mining runs securely in the background while active. Users can start or stop mining at any time without risk to the system.

Earnings Distribution

When mining is stopped, 70% of the total mined rewards are credited directly to the user's account balance.

Team Reward Allocation

The remaining 30% is distributed automatically to the team structure: Level 1 receives 17%, Level 2 receives 8%, and Level 3 receives 5%.

Upline Eligibility Rule

To receive downline mining profit, an upline must maintain a sufficient active balance in their account. If the required balance condition is not met, the upline will not be eligible to receive commission from downline mining rewards.
